# 多样化模型参考样例

这些提示词只用于生成 Benchmark 的参考真值候选，不属于无模型算法运行链路。每次生成应保存原始提示词、模型版本、日期和人工评分；参考图不能直接混入训练姿态库。

## R1 实景山谷桥梁

Photorealistic mountain valley at golden hour; replace one winding stone footbridge with a single continuous Nailong following the bridge curve; preserve perspective, vegetation, shadows and atmospheric depth; integrated natural lighting; no pasted PNG or flat yellow blob.

## R2 东亚市集街拍

Candid East Asian market photograph at dusk; replace one central vendor with a coherent Nailong preserving standing pose, apron silhouette, hand gesture, crowd occlusion, wet reflections, lens rendering and film grain; no sticker look.

## P1 文艺复兴式人像

Seated fictional museum portrait in Renaissance oil style; replace the whole seated figure with one coherent Nailong preserving folded hands, clothing silhouette, chiaroscuro, glazing and canvas grain; painted into the canvas, not a 3D cutout.

## P2 中国水墨长卷

Classical Chinese ink-and-color handscroll; transform the main mountain ridge into a continuous winding Nailong and distant ridges into smaller forms; preserve ink wash, negative space, mineral blue-green palette and brush texture.
